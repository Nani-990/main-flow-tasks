Maruti Suzuki Baleno Features

Explore the Key Features of the Maruti Suzuki Baleno

1.2L Petrol Engine

The Baleno is powered by a highly efficient 1.2L petrol engine, offering excellent performance and fuel efficiency.

LED Projector Headlamps

Modern LED projector headlamps provide better illumination and enhance the vehicle's overall appeal.

SmartPlay Studio

The SmartPlay Studio infotainment system supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, providing seamless connectivity.

Dual Airbags

Standard dual airbags for both driver and passenger ensure enhanced safety during every drive.

Spacious Interior

The cabin offers ample legroom and storage space for a comfortable driving experience.

Efficient Mileage

With fuel-efficient technology, the Baleno offers great mileage on both city roads and highways.
