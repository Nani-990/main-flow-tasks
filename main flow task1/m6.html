Maruti Suzuki Vitara Brezza Features

Explore the Key Features of the Maruti Suzuki Vitara Brezza

1.5L Petrol Engine

The Vitara Brezza comes with a 1.5L petrol engine providing excellent power and performance for a compact SUV.

LED Projector Headlamps

Advanced LED projector headlamps ensure excellent visibility, adding to the vehicle's sleek and modern look.

SmartPlay Studio

The SmartPlay Studio infotainment system offers smartphone integration, navigation, and voice recognition features.

Dual Airbags & ABS

Safety is a priority, with dual airbags and Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) to protect the driver and passengers.

High Ground Clearance

The Vitara Brezza's high ground clearance ensures smooth driving on rough terrains, perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

Modern Design

The exterior boasts a bold design, making it a standout vehicle in its category.
