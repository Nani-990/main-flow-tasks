Maruti Suzuki Dzire Features

Explore the Key Features of the Maruti Suzuki Dzire

1.2L Petrol Engine

The Dzire comes with a powerful 1.2L petrol engine that provides a smooth and responsive drive.

Touchscreen Infotainment System

The Dzire is equipped with a high-quality touchscreen infotainment system that supports Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.

LED Projector Headlamps

These headlamps not only look stylish but also provide excellent visibility while driving at night.

ABS with EBD

The Dzire is equipped with Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) with Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD) for added safety.

Dual Airbags

Dual airbags come standard to protect the driver and passengers in case of a collision.

Elegant Design

The Dzire is designed to offer a stylish and comfortable experience with its sleek exterior and luxurious interior.
